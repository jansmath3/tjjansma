Pioneer Case Study
Go to Site
Summary:

This project required extremely minimalist design with sparse information.

Requirements:
  1. 3 day turn around
  2. Extremely minimalist design
Problem:

How to build a website in 3 days with little information to display

Solution:

Pioneer Strategy is a marketing firm that works primarily in the education sector that needed a website. Their largest problem was that they were dealing with how much or little they wanted to advertise in order to satisfy all of their clients. Ultimately, they decided on creating an extremely minimalist website. Based on an extremely short timeframe for completion, I came up with the idea that site look like a simple business card. The idea went over well and I was able to put the HTML website together quickly to the satisfaction of the customer.

Outcome:

Design simplicity made this an easy project. This website is practical and gets the message its owners wanted across effectively.
